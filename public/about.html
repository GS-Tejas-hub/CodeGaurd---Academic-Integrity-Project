About CodeGuard

Revolutionizing academic integrity with cutting-edge AI technology

Our Mission

At CodeGuard, we believe in the power of authentic learning and genuine innovation. Our mission is to protect academic integrity while fostering a culture of original thinking and creativity in educational institutions worldwide.


We understand the challenges educators face in today's digital age, where students have unprecedented access to code repositories, AI tools, and online resources. Our advanced AI-powered platform helps teachers maintain high academic standards while providing students with the tools they need to develop their skills authentically.


By combining state-of-the-art machine learning algorithms with comprehensive code analysis, we provide educators with the confidence they need to assess student work fairly and accurately.

AI-Powered Detection

Advanced machine learning algorithms that can detect AI-generated content with 95%+ accuracy, ensuring authentic student work.

Multi-Source Analysis

Comprehensive search across GitHub, Stack Overflow, and web sources to identify copied code from multiple platforms.

Detailed Reports

Generate comprehensive PDF reports with evidence, similarity scores, and actionable insights for educators.

Lightning Fast

Analyze entire repositories in minutes, not hours. Get results quickly without compromising accuracy.

Secure & Private

Educator Friendly
